About us

We make cakes with love
Elegant Bakery is a family-owned business run by Janusz Zrodlowski and his wife Bozena.
Janusz, Executive Pastry Chef and Owner, has been pleasing customers with custom wedding cakes, tortes, and fine pastries since 1972. Chef Janusz was trained in Europe under several master chefs in some of Europe's finest hotels.
In 1985, Chef Janusz received a Master Pastry Chef Certificate and has since won several awards for excellence at local, regional, and national pastry competitions. In 1988, he came to America to continue his training and to expand his skills. Since then, he has worked in many top line 5 star hotels across the United States. Janusz instills an old family tradition of classical European style in each of his recipes. He also had the privilege to present his wonderful work to many significant guests such as our Presidents, Senators, and various celebrities.
